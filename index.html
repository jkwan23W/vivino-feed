Maison Fang

The Story

Photo Reference - Vineyard Estate

Between tradition and precision, Maison FANG is the story of an enthusiast dedicated to the creation of exceptional wines and the extraction of the vine's pure soul. Founded by Ming FANG, whose Chinese roots intertwine with French soil, the domaine creates wines of rare authenticity and unparalleled excellence.

MAISON FANG embodies the essence of Burgundy, where respect for terroir and ancestral know-how converge. Every bottle that leaves the domaine is the result of a relentless quest for perfection, bridging continents through a shared love for the vine.
